# Introduction

Welcome to the Bodhi Central documentation. This is the centralized hub for guidelines, documentation, and technical information about the Bodhi Central project.

## What is Bodhi Central?

Bodhi Central is a non-profit organization dedicated to the preservation and dissemination of the Buddha's teachings. We are a group of volunteers and professionals who are passionate about the Dharma, and we are committed to making it accessible to everyone.

## Project Overview

Bodhi Central is a web application that provides access to a collection of Buddhist texts and learning environments. It is a work in progress, and we are always looking for volunteers and professionals to help us with our mission.
